Advanced Materials for Every Application

We use a range of materials—each chosen for its specific role in the building process.

Core Materials:

  • Thermoplastics: PLA, ABS, PETG for durability & precision

Circular Design
▼

Closing the Loop

Our furniture is designed to stay in circulation—reclaimed, reprinted, and repurposed instead of discarded.

Circular Approach:

  • Recyclable plastics (PLA, PETG, recycled polymers)
  • Bio-based materials reduce environmental impact
  • 3D printing eliminates excess scrap
  • Old furniture transforms into new forms
